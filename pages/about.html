Competence


Kooperieren

The ÖGI maintains cooperation with scientific associations, universities, working groups and societies in Austria and abroad. Furthermore, with the legal professional associations and the university hospitals responsible for the subject as well as other institutions involved in continuing education.

Research

One of the concerns of the ÖGI is scientific work on the subject of implantology. Scientific work is encouraged and supported in order to be permanently at the cutting edge of science. Scientific journals are also supported for this purpose.

Further education

The transfer of know-how to members takes place within the framework of scientific conferences, meetings, lectures, continuing education courses and seminars. All current offers of events can be found here.

Treat

Over 500 medical specialists are members of ÖGI and meet the highest quality standards. They benefit from the scientific know-how of the society and offer their patients expertise based on the latest findings.
